Selected Case Study精選案例

Legal AI RAG — Taiwan Market 法律 AI RAG——台灣市場

A Taiwanese legal-tech CEO invited me to evaluate a RAG architecture for Taiwan's 21M court judgements (ahead of their Series C). My read after consulting a managing partner: the TAM is constrained — a civil-law system with ~10,000 lawyers implies a ~US$3M market at a 1% payroll take rate. The architecture below stands regardless.

一位台灣法律科技公司的 CEO 邀我評估針對台灣 2,100 萬筆判決書的 RAG 架構（為其 C 輪募資準備）。 與一位資深合夥人討論後，我的判斷是：TAM 受限——大陸法系、約 1 萬名律師，以 1% 薪資抽成估算， 市場規模約 300 萬美元。但下列架構本身仍然成立。

1 · Data Strategy & Knowledge Graph1 · 資料策略與知識圖譜

  • Entity-centric parsing: extract each case's fact-pattern — who, when, where, what happened, and the outcome — rather than treating judgments as raw text.
  • 以實體為核心的解析：萃取每件判決的事實模式——人、時、地、事、結果——而非把判決當原始文字處理。
  • Vector–keyword mapping: vectorize the keywords, not the raw text, mapping semantic concepts to specific records. A citation graph links judgments into a legal knowledge graph.
  • 向量—關鍵字映射：向量化的是關鍵字而非原文，把語意概念對應到具體判決；再以引用關係建立法律知識圖譜。

2 · Hybrid RAG & Cost Control2 · 混合式 RAG 與成本控制

  • Hybrid retrieval: match intent against the keyword vector map + Elasticsearch exact match; the LLM only processes the Top-N to control token burn.
  • 混合式檢索：以關鍵字向量映射＋Elasticsearch 精確比對來匹配意圖；LLM 僅處理 Top-N 結果以控制 token 消耗。
  • Smart caching: pre-computed summaries for frequent cases drive repeat cost toward zero.
  • 智慧快取：對高頻案件預先計算摘要，讓重複查詢的成本趨近於零。

3 · Why it works3 · 為什麼有效

  • Cost-first engineering: vectorizing keywords instead of raw text cuts storage and embedding cost dramatically; hybrid lookup + caching means cost-per-query falls as usage grows.
  • 成本優先的工程：向量化關鍵字而非原文，大幅降低儲存與嵌入成本；混合查詢＋快取讓單次查詢成本隨用量上升而下降。
  • Zero training data & portable: a real-time knowledge base with no fine-tuning, plus a Learning-to-Rank loop on user clicks — and the "extract → map → RAG" pipeline is market-agnostic.
  • 零訓練資料、可移植：無需微調的即時知識庫，搭配以使用者點擊回饋的 Learning-to-Rank 迴圈；「萃取 → 映射 → RAG」流程不限市場。
